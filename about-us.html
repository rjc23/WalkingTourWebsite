About Us (Me)


Hi, I'm Ryan, the tour guide that will show you around Adelaide! I am a student at The University of Adelaide. While I am not studying, I love showing people around this beautiful city.



Your tour guide, Ryan

First tour ever in 2016!



The first tour to ever depart was on 25th October 2016. I’d been back from living in Spain/travelling Europe trying to settle back into life in Adelaide. I had been on so many free ‘tips based’ walking tours while travelling around Europe. After a bit of research, I found out that no one operated walking tours of that kind here in Adelaide. There were others in other cities in Australia but not here. I already had a passion for Adelaide and teaching experience so I decided to print a couple of flyers, make a shirt and give the tours a go! Since then, I changed from “Adelaide Free Walking Tour” to “Tours Around Adelaide” and I’ve had over 5000 people come and discover Adelaide with me.


I think Adelaide is a beautiful city so why don't you come along and I'll show you why! See you on a tour soon! Ryan.
