Free Tour?

The free walking tour concept started in 2005 in Berlin. Instead of tours having up front costs, participants are invited to come along on the tour, and at the end, if they think the tour is worth their time and money they can pay what they think it is worth. If your guide is awesome, give them an awesome tip, if not, you don't have to give them anything. You will never be pressured to tip! Free walking tours now operate in 105 cities around the world, mostly by independent local operators, just like this one!
