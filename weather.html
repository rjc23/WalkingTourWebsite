Walking Tour Weather Policy

​

For safety reasons, Tours are cancelled if it is forecast to be 38 degrees celsius or over. I check the Bureau of Meteorology website.



Tours can also be cancelled if a weather warning has been issued for Adelaide/SA. Warnings can be for strong winds, hail, wild weather etc. Warnings are posted here.

​

As temperatures/conditions regularly change, I will check the weather the day before the tour, if it is forecast to be 38 degrees or over the tour will be cancelled. Emails and text messages will be sent to all those who have booked in.
